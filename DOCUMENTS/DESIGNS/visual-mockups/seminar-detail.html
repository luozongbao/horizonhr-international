About This Seminar

The 2026 Thailand Career Fair is the premier live streaming event connecting Thai students, graduates, and professionals with leading Chinese enterprises actively hiring Southeast Asian bilingual talent.

This seminar features presentations from 15 participating companies including technology firms, financial institutions, healthcare companies, and manufacturing enterprises — all with active job openings for Thai nationals with Chinese language skills.

Whether you're a fresh graduate from a Chinese university or a working professional looking to advance your career in China, this event gives you direct access to HR directors and decision makers from top employers.
