Who We Are

Hubei Horizon International Talent Service Co., Ltd. (湖北豪睿国际人才服务有限公司) is a leading international talent recruitment and education consulting company headquartered in Wuhan, China.

We specialize in connecting Southeast Asian students and professionals with Chinese universities and enterprises, providing a full-service platform for education consulting, talent recruitment, interview facilitation, and career development.

Our bilingual team of over 50 professionals serves clients in China, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia, and beyond.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our company history

2018 — Founded
Hubei Horizon International established in Wuhan with a mission to connect Southeast Asian students with Chinese universities.
2020 — Enterprise Division
Launched enterprise talent recruitment services, connecting bilingual graduates with Chinese and international companies.
2022 — Digital Platform
Launched online interview platform and seminar system, enabling remote talent assessment for 10,000+ concurrent viewers.
2024 — Regional Expansion
Expanded operations to Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia with local partner offices in Bangkok, Ho Chi Minh City, and Jakarta.
2026 — HBHR Platform Launch
Launched the fully integrated HBHR digital platform with AI-powered matching, TRTC video interviews, and multi-language support.
